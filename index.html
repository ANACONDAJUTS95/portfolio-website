Hi there! I'm Anthon Chris Ellys S. Sepacio, a passionate software developer and UI/UX designer hailing from Trece Martires City, Cavite. I recently earned my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Santo Tomas, where I proudly made the Dean's List several times. My journey has been filled with exciting projects, from leading a software engineering team to creating innovative thesis work. I love diving into various programming languages and frameworks like AngularJS, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, .NET ASP, and more, balancing my tech skills with a knack for crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces using tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Figma.
My professional path took an exciting turn during my internship at Analog Devices, where I worked as a UI/UX Designer and Front End Developer. There, I developed a system to monitor work and test bench utilization, diving deep into technologies like AngularJS and ASP .NET. I’m always eager to learn and grow, as evidenced by my certifications in Responsive Web Design and Front End Development Libraries from FreeCodeCamp. Outside of coding and designing, I’ve been active in various organizations, serving as Chief of Staff and Director of Digital Productions for the Computer Science Society, and leading the Audio Visual Production Division for UST Volunteers for UNICEF. This blend of technical skills, leadership experience, and community involvement makes my journey in the tech world both dynamic and fulfilling.
